parameterTerms
The available terms used as parameters for searching. The available terms are as follows:
  • POIS: poi;
  • EVENTS: event;
  • ROUTES: route;
  • SEARCH_PARAMETERS:
    • BASE: base;
    • ID: id;
    • CATEGORY: category;
    • TAG: tag;
    • COMPLETE: complete;
    • MINIMAL: minimal;
    • COORDS: coords;
    • CODE: code;
    • OFFSET: offset;
    • LIMIT: limit;
    • TIME: time;
    • NAME: name;
    • RELATION: relation;
    • LIST: list.
An example of its usage: parameterTerms.SEARCH_PARAMETERS.TAG
Defined in: jquery.citysdk-tourism.stub.js.
resourceTerms
The available terms used to access a given resource in a TourismClient stub. The available terms are as follows:
  • RESOURCE_POIS:
    • FIND_POI: find-poi;
    • FIND_POI_RELATION: find-poi-relation.
  • RESOURCE_EVENT:
    • FIND_EVENT: find-event;
    • FIND_EVENT_RELATION: find-event-relation.
  • RESOURCE_ROUTE:
    • FIND_ROUTE: find-route;
    • FIND_ROUTE_RELATION: find-route-relation.
  • FIND_CATEGORIES: find-categories;
  • FIND_TAGS: find-tags;
  • FIND_CODE: find-code.
An example of its usage: parameterTerms.RESOURCE_POIS.FIND_POI
Defined in: jquery.citysdk-tourism.stub.js.
