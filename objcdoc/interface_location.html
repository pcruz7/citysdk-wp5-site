Detailed Description

Representation of the Location class of the UML diagram found in: http://www.w3.org/2010/POI/documents/Core/core-20111216.html#poi-data-model.

A Location is composed of:

  • a group of GeoPoint, Line, Polygon representing the Location's Geometry (such as: entry/exit points);
  • a group of Relationship representing the relationship of this Location with other PointOfInterest or Event;
  • address representing the civic address of the Location in vCard format.
